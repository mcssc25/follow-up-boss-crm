More Than Music

The Wharf is a year-round destination — not just on concert nights.

🎡

Ferris Wheel

The iconic 112-foot observation wheel offers panoramic views of the Intracoastal Waterway, the marina, and the Orange Beach skyline. A must-do, day or night.

⛵

Marina & Dining

A full-service marina surrounded by waterfront restaurants. Dock your boat, grab fresh seafood, and watch the sunset over the harbor.

🛍

Shopping & Boutiques

Unique coastal boutiques, surf shops, art galleries, and specialty stores. Browse locally owned shops you won't find anywhere else.

🎬

Movies & Entertainment

Catch the latest movies, play mini golf, or just stroll the boardwalk. There's always something going on at The Wharf for every age group.

Ready to Live Where You Vacation?
