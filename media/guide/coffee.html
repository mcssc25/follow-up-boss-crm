Happy Pappy's Coffeehouse

LOCAL OWNED

Gulf Shores Hwy 59 · Coffee & Croissants

The quintessential Gulf Shores local spot. Amazing fresh-baked croissants, strong local roasts, and an atmosphere that feels like a home away from home. Their lavender latte is a cult favorite.

Southern Grind Coffee House

THE WHARF

Orange Beach · Hotel Indigo

With two locations (The Wharf and Hotel Indigo), the Southern Grind combines gourmet coffee with a gorgeous upscale home decor boutique. The perfect spot to grab a drink and find local coastal art.

Bad Ass Coffee of Hawaii

BEACHSIDE

Gulf Shores · Beach Access

Located right near the main public beach in Gulf Shores. Specialized in Hawaiian Kona blends. It's the go-to for many visitors before they set up their chairs for a long day on the sand.
