GULF COAST ADVENTURES

Dolphin Cruises in
Gulf Shores & Orange Beach

Get out on the water and see bottlenose dolphins in their natural habitat. One of the most popular activities on the Alabama Gulf Coast — and one the whole family will remember.

1.5 – 3 hrs

Trip Duration

Most cruises run between an hour and a half and three hours, depending on the tour type.

$30 – $400

Price Range

From affordable shared cruises to private charters for the whole family. Pricing depends on group size and tour type.

Guaranteed

Dolphin Sightings

Most operators guarantee you will see dolphins or your next trip is free. These waters are home to year-round pods.
