AFTER DARK ON THE GULF COAST

Nightlife & Live Music

From legendary beach bars to laid-back waterfront stages, the Gulf Coast comes alive after sunset. Cold drinks, warm nights, and live music seven days a week.

Where to Go Out

The bars, stages, and hangouts that keep the coast alive after dark.

Flora-Bama Lounge

LEGENDARY

AL/FL State Line · 5 Stages

World-famous beach bar straddling the Alabama-Florida state line. Live entertainment 365 days a year across 5 stages — country, rock, dance, and beach music. Home of the annual Mullet Toss. A bucket-list destination.

florabama.com →

The Hangout

FAMILY FUN

Gulf Shores · Live Music & Events

Great food meets nonstop entertainment. Live music, fire pit, foam parties, and a resident DJ. Home of the Sand in My Boots music festival. Fun for all ages during the day, energetic party scene at night.

The Ice House

LOCAL BAR

Orange Beach · Casual

A local favorite where the drinks are cold and the vibe is real. No frills, no tourist traps — just a solid neighborhood bar where locals unwind. The kind of place you become a regular at.

LuLu's

LIVE MUSIC

Gulf Shores · Nightly Music

Lucy Buffett's iconic spot on the Intracoastal Waterway. Nightly live music spanning country, blues, and indie folk on the waterfront stage. Grab a L.A. (Lower Alabama) Bushwacker and settle in for the show.

Tacky Jacks

MULTI-LOCATION

Gulf Shores · Orange Beach · Fort Morgan

Live local and regional artists plus karaoke nights across three locations. Laid-back waterfront atmosphere with cold drinks and good times. A coast staple with something happening every night of the week.

Sandshaker at The Wharf

WHARF

Orange Beach · The Wharf

Located at The Wharf entertainment district. Live music Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday nights with karaoke on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Great cocktails and a party atmosphere right on the marina.

Ready to Live Where You Vacation?

Live music every night, world-class beach bars, and sunsets that never get old. Imagine calling the Gulf Coast home.
