Why People Are Choosing Gulf Shores

Everything You Love About the Beach.
None of What You Don't.

Gulf Shores and Orange Beach offer the coastal lifestyle without the tourist-trap prices, the overcrowding, or the pretension.

A Real Community

This isn't a transient resort town. Gulf Shores has a year-round community of families, retirees, and small business owners who chose to be here. Neighbors wave. People know your name at the coffee shop.

Your Dollar Goes Further

Cost of living runs 20–40% below major metros. Property taxes are among the lowest in the nation. Social Security and pensions are tax-free in Alabama. Your money simply goes further here.

The Family Magnet

Your kids may have moved away — but give them a reason to come back. A beach house on the Gulf is the kind of place grandkids grow up talking about. Holiday dinners hit different with a sunset view.

Easy to Get To

Gulf Shores International Airport (JKA) offers regional flights right in town. Pensacola International (PNS) is 45 minutes away with direct flights from most major cities. Drive from Atlanta in 5 hours, Nashville in 7, Dallas in 8.

Top-Rated Healthcare Nearby

South Baldwin Regional Medical Center is local. USA Health and Sacred Heart in Pensacola are a short drive. Quality care without big-city hassle.

Sugar-White Sand, Year-Round

Consistently rated among the best beaches in America. The sand is pure quartz crystal — cool to the touch even in summer. Winters are mild enough for beach walks in January.

Things to Actually Do

World-class fishing, golf courses, The Wharf entertainment district, fresh seafood, kayaking, and a growing food scene. You won't be bored — unless you want to be.
