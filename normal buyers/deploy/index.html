Financing Your Home

The Loan to Match the Neighborhood

Dave Davis is both your Realtor and a licensed Lending Specialist. Every subdivision above fits a different loan product — here's the quick map.

C

Conventional

The default for primary residences and second homes. 3–20% down, strong terms for buyers with solid credit. Works for most subdivisions on this page.

F

FHA

Low 3.5% down payment and more flexible credit requirements. A strong path to first-time homeownership in communities like Crimson Ridge, Gulf Pines, and Raley Farms.

V

VA

For active-duty, veterans, and qualifying spouses. 0% down, no PMI, and competitive rates. Gulf Shores has a large military community — we close VA loans constantly.

U

USDA

0% down financing in designated rural areas. Several North Gulf Shores and Bon Secour subdivisions still qualify — a hidden gem for eligible buyers.
