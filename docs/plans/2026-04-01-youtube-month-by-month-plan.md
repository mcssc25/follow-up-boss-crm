## Pre-Launch Checklist (Complete Before Filming Anything)

### Channel Setup (Day 1-2)
- [ ] Create or claim YouTube channel
- [ ] Set custom handle (e.g., @BigBeachAL — short, memorable, brandable)
- [ ] Write channel description (250+ words, keyword-loaded):
  > "Your insider guide to living on Alabama's Gulf Coast. We cover Gulf Shores, Orange Beach, Fairhope, Daphne, Spanish Fort, Foley, and all of Baldwin County, Alabama. Whether you're moving to the Gulf Coast, investing in beach condos, or just dreaming about the beach life — we've got you covered. New videos every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. Subscribe for relocation guides, condo tours, restaurant reviews, market updates, and the real story of what it's like to live here year-round."
- [ ] Add links: website, Instagram, Facebook, email, phone
- [ ] Set channel keywords in YouTube Studio > Settings > Channel: "Gulf Shores Alabama, Orange Beach Alabama, living in Gulf Shores, moving to Alabama, Baldwin County real estate, beach condos Alabama, Gulf Coast living"

### Branding Assets (Day 2-3)
- [ ] Channel profile photo — professional headshot or logo (800x800px)
- [ ] Channel banner — you + the beach + tagline (2560x1440px, safe area 1546x423px)
  - Include: posting schedule ("New videos Mon/Wed/Fri"), social handles, tagline
- [ ] Create 3-4 thumbnail templates in Canva:
  - **Template A — "Moving To" videos**: Beach background, your face (reaction), bold location text
  - **Template B — Property/Condo tours**: Property photo, your face, "TOUR" or price text
  - **Template C — Lifestyle/Local**: Vibrant food/sunset/event photo, text overlay
  - **Template D — Market/Data**: Chart graphic, concerned/excited face, numbers
- [ ] Channel trailer script (60-90 seconds — film this on the beach):
  > "Hey, I'm [Name] and this is [Channel Name]. If you've ever thought about moving to Alabama's Gulf Coast — Gulf Shores, Orange Beach, Fairhope, or anywhere in Baldwin County — you're in the right place. Every week we do condo tours, restaurant reviews, neighborhood guides, and honest talk about what it's actually like to live here. Not the tourist version — the real deal. Hit subscribe and I'll see you in the next one."

### Equipment Purchases (Day 1-3)
- [ ] DJI Mini 4 Pro drone (~$760) — this is the #1 differentiator vs. competitors
- [ ] Wireless mic: DJI Mic 2 (~$250) or Rode Wireless Go II (~$300)
- [ ] Small LED light panel for indoor shots (~$30-50, Amazon)
- [ ] Phone tripod/gimbal: DJI OM 7 (~$150) or basic tripod (~$25)

- [ ] Create a shared Google Sheet or Notion board for content calendar

## The Non-Negotiable Rules

1. **Never miss a posting day.** 3x/week minimum. Consistency beats everything.
2. **First 5 seconds hook.** If you don't grab them immediately, they're gone.
3. **Reply to every comment within 24 hours.** This is how you build community AND signal engagement to the algorithm.
4. **Film B-roll constantly.** Every time you're at the beach, a restaurant, driving around — film 30 seconds. You'll use it all.
5. **Check analytics weekly.** Know your numbers. Double down on what works.
6. **Don't chase viral.** Chase search traffic. Viral fades in 48 hours. Search compounds forever.
7. **Be patient through the dead zone.** Months 1-4 will feel like shouting into the void. This is where every competitor quit. Push through it.
8. **Be genuinely honest.** Talk about hurricanes, humidity, insurance costs, tourist traffic. Honesty is what separates you from every agent who only talks about sunsets.
9. **Every video should answer a question someone is actually Googling.** If you can't identify the search query, don't make the video.
10. **The goal is leads, not subscribers.** 500 subscribers who are actively considering a move are worth more than 50,000 subscribers who just like beach videos.

## Quick Reference: Video Formula

### Structure for Every Long-Form Video

```
0:00 - HOOK (5-10 sec)
       "If you're thinking about moving to [City], there are [X] things you need to know first."

0:10 - INTRO (15-30 sec)
       Quick channel intro, subscribe reminder. Keep it fast.

0:30 - CONTENT (8-15 min)
       Deliver the goods. Use location footage, drone shots, screen grabs.
       Break into clear sections with on-screen text.

X:00 - PERSONAL TAKE (1-2 min)
       "Here's what I honestly think..." — this is where you build trust.

X:00 - CTA (15-30 sec)
       "If you're serious about making the move, I put together a free
       relocation guide — link's in the description. And if you found this
```
